AmigaVision for Raspberry Pi

AmigaVision runs well on any Raspberry Pi 5, 500, 500+, or CM5 with 1GB of RAM — or more, but 1GB is sufficient. Raspberry Pi 4 is also supported, but too slow for the most accurate emulation.

- Use Raspberry Pi Imager to flash the image to an SD card — a 16GB card is the minimum size.
- Insert into your Raspberry Pi and enjoy!

PS: Default resolution is 1080p, but you can change this in RePlayOS settings — press the Home key or the Windows key to bring up the menu.
